## Assessment

### Knowledge Check (5 min)

**Question 1:** What happens if you try to approve your own leave request?  
**Expected Answer:** SoD blocks it - someone else must approve

**Question 2:** As a Head, what can you approve?  
**Expected Answer:** Team members' requests (first-level), not your own

**Question 3:** As an Officer, can you final approve your work?  
**Expected Answer:** No - someone else must approve

**Question 4:** What does "(SoD)" label on a button mean?  
**Expected Answer:** You're blocked because you created/initiated the record

**Question 5:** Who can bypass SoD?  
**Expected Answer:** Only Admin and Super Admin
